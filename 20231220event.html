SAKANA Family Event

January

  • 1: New Year's day
    At about 1AM, We visit a shrine to pray there and greet neighbors.
  • 1-3: go to grandparents' house.
    we go both in mother's and father's side to greet.
  • 5-10: ski
    someday in between 5-10, we go skiing.

February

  • 3: begining of spring
    We celebrate the beginning of spring. We eat long-rolled sushi that day. Father wears a demon's mask, and we throw beans at him to ward off the bad spirits in the house.
  • 14: Valentine's Day
    Unlike in the US, usually girls give chocolate to boys in Japan. My mother and I make chocolate cakes together and give them to father and grandfathers.

March

  • 14: White day
    In Japan, boys give presents to the girls who gave presents on Valentine's day in return. My father and grandparents take us to dinner or cook for us.

May

  • 4: Greenery Day
    It is a national holiday due to the emperor's love for nature. Our family tend to do small travel to walk in the nature. Typically we go to Kyoto.
  • 24: Kanna's birthday
    Natsu and Sato cook for me and eat dinner and dessert that Kanna makes.

July

  • 7: Star Festival
    It is said that only this day can a star princess and a star prince meet up on the Milky Way. We write our wishes on pieces of paper and tie them to bamboo trees.
  • 28: Sato's birthday
    Natsu and Kanna plan this beforehand and celebrate his birthday. We usually make shortcake becuase that is his favorite cake.

August

  • 8: Sato's father's birthday.
    We visit him and take him to dinner or take out his favorite meal and give him presents.

September

  • 9: Natsu's mother's birthday.
    We visit her and cook for her dinner and give presents and cake we make.
