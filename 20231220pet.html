Meet My Pets

My Beetle, GABU

In 2010, my father came back with the beetle on his back.
Natsu and Kanna usually dislike insects but that beetle attructed us, so we decided to welcom him.
we named him GABU from the onomatopoeia of the biting sound in Japanese.
He lived at least a year, which is regarded as a long life for a beetle. We were sad, but we were happy that we had a good time with him.

My Fish, KIYO and YOSHI

One day, the neighbors of my grandparents gave me two fish, each a male and a female Oryzias.
The neighbor's name was KIYOSHI, so I named him and her KIYO and YOSHI.
I bred them and they had many fish but the parent fish ate them so only 8 fish survived. I adored them and took care of them.
However, I started to go to junior high school, and I started to be busy because of schoolwork and tennis club practice,
so I released them in the river after consulting with the neighbor and the biology teacher in the school.
